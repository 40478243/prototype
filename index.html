About Us

Napier Quiz Book was created in order to provide educational quizes to improve their knowledge of the world. The particular quiz above is our first quiz and will test the users knowledge of geography, countries and the capital cities of that countries. These quizes contain a 30 second timer and will show the right answer one the users answer has been selected in order to provide opportunity for learning. Napier Quiz Book is part of a project created for Edinburgh Napier University for the module 'Web Technologies', as part of BEng (Hons) Cybersecurity & Forensics.
